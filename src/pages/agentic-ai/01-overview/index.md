### 💡 Key Concept: The "Loop"
Unlike standard LLMs that generate a single response and stop, Agentic systems operate in a loop:
1. **Perceive:** Take in the environment or user request.
2. **Reason:** Plan the next step using Chain-of-Thought or similar patterns.
3. **Act:** Execute a tool, call an API, or provide a final response.
