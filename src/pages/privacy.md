**Last Updated:** April 2026

Kavriq is an educational website. This page explains what limited information may be collected when you visit the site.

## Analytics

Kavriq uses Google Analytics to understand aggregate website traffic, such as which pages are visited, what devices and browsers are used, and how visitors arrive at the site.

Google Analytics is only loaded after you choose **Accept analytics** in the consent banner. If you choose **Reject**, the Google Analytics tag is not loaded.

Your consent choice is stored in your browser using local storage so the banner does not appear on every page view.

## Advertising

Kavriq does not use Google Analytics for advertising personalization.

The site sets Google consent mode advertising signals to denied, including ad storage, ad user data, and ad personalization.

## Cookies and Local Storage

Kavriq may use local storage for site preferences, such as theme selection and analytics consent.

Google Analytics may use cookies or similar browser storage only if you accept analytics.

## Third-Party Services

If analytics is accepted, Google Analytics may process usage data according to Google's own privacy terms and policies.

External links on Kavriq may take you to third-party websites. Their privacy practices are controlled by those websites, not by Kavriq.

## Changes

This privacy policy may be updated as the site evolves or as analytics and privacy requirements change.

## Contact

For privacy questions about Kavriq, contact Ravi Shankar Kumar through the links on the site.
