Welcome to **ML Essentials**. This isn't just a collection of tutorials; it is a structured curriculum designed to help you build a mental model of machine learning from the bottom up. 

We avoid the "black box" approach. Instead, we look at the engine—the linear algebra, the optimization loops, and the architectural decisions—that makes modern AI run.

---

## The Paved Path

- [Introduction](/ml-essentials/introduction) — High-level overview and the "Why" behind ML.
- [Getting Started](/ml-essentials/getting-started) — Environment setup and first steps.
- [Maths for AI/ML](/ml-essentials/maths-for-aiml) — Linear Algebra, Calculus, and Probability.
- [Core ML](/ml-essentials/core-ml) — Classical algorithms and statistical learning.
- [Deep Learning](/ml-essentials/deep-learning) — Neural networks, CNNs, and RNNs.
- [Practical ML](/ml-essentials/practical-ml) — Preprocessing, Tuning, and Deployment.
- [Advanced ML](/ml-essentials/advanced) — GenAI, Bayesian methods, and specialized domains.
- [Projects](/ml-essentials/projects) — End-to-end hands-on case studies.
- [Resources](/ml-essentials/resources) — Libraries, reading lists, and community.

---

### How to Navigate
* **For Beginners:** Follow the paved path numerically. Each module builds the vocabulary needed for the next.
* **For Practitioners:** Jump into the **Maths** section for a first-principles refresher or the **Deep Learning** modules to see how modern LLMs are structured.

> **AUTH Note:** This curriculum is a living document. As the field moves from static models to Agentic Systems, we update these modules to ensure you are learning with the advantage of hindsight.
