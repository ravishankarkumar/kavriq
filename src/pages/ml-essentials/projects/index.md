### Project Guidelines
Each project includes:
1.  **Problem Statement:** Understanding the business or research goal.
2.  **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):** Visualizing patterns and outliers.
3.  **Modeling:** Selecting and training the appropriate algorithm.
4.  **Evaluation:** Metrics that actually matter for the specific use case.
